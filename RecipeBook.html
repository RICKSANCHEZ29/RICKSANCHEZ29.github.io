Recipe #1 : Pizza Dough

  • Time: 60 Minutes
  • Serves: 8 People
Ingredients Quantity
Water, sugar, yeast, flour, olive oil, salt 2&1/2 cups, 1 tsp, 2 tsp, 7 cups, 6 tsp, 1&1/2

Step 1: Sprinle sugar and yeast in warm water and let it sit until bubbles start to form.

Step 2: Combine flour and salt, add oil in the middle. Stir until mix becomes doughy.

Step 3:Knead the dough for about 10-15 minutes.

Step 4:Put into a bowl and let it ride for up to 24 hours.

Step 5:Punch thr dough down and knead for another minute and then cut the dough into 4 equal parts.

Recipe #2: Lasagna

  • Time: 195 Minutes
  • Serves: 12 People
Ingredients Quantity
Sausage, Ground Beef, Minced Onion, Crushed Garlic, Tomatoes, Tomato Paste, Tomato Sauce, Water, Sugar, Basil, Seeds, Seasoing, Salt, Pepper, Noodles, Ricota Cheese, Eggs, Mozzarella Cheese, Parmesan Cheese 1 lb, 3/4 lb, 1/2 cup, 2 cloves, 28 oz, 12oz, 13oz, 1/2 cup, 2tsp, 3/2 tsp, 1/2 tsp, 1 tsp, 3/2 tsp, 1/4 tsp, 4tbsp, 12 noodles, 16oz, 1 egg, 3/4 lb, 3/4 cup

Step 1: In a Dutch oven, cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Season with sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, 1 teaspoon salt, pepper, and 2 tablespoons parsley. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.

Step 2: Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.

Step 3: Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

Step 4: To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with one half of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with a third of mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray, or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.

Step 5: Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.

Source: Homemade Lasagna

Recipe #3: Cuban Sandwhich

  • Time: 20 Minutes
  • Serves: 8 People
Ingredients Quantity
Pork Tenderloin, Cuban Bread, Swiss Chesse, Smoked Ham, Dill Pickles, Mustard, Butter 1 Bread, 1lb, 1lb, 1 cup, 1/4 cup, 2tbsp

Step 1: Slice the loaf of bread in half lengthwise and then cut into 4 equal parts.

Step 2: Begin layering the ingredients by adding half of the cheese to the bottom layer of the sandwich bread. Top with the smoked ham, sliced pork, and pickles. Place the remaining cheese on top. Spread mustard on the top piece of bread and place on the sandwich.

Step 3: Melt the butter in a skillet or griddle over medium heat. Add the sandwiches and set a heavy skillet, such as an iron skillet, on top of the sandwiches to press them down. Once toasted flip the sandwich and repeat.

Step 4: Slice the sandwiches in half before servings.

Source: Cuban Sandwhich
